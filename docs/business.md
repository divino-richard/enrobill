# Senior High School Enrollment Process

### Step 1: Submission of Requirements

1. Proceed to the *Registrar* to secure and accomplish the enrollment form.
2. Submit all required documents and supporting requirements for verification.
3. Wait for confirmation that all submitted requirements are complete.

### Step 2: Payment at the Cashier

* Students who are *not covered by the Senior High School Voucher Program* are required to pay a *minimum registration fee of ₱150.00* for ID processing.
* Students who are *qualified for the voucher program* are generally *not required to pay a down payment* during enrollment.


**Note:** Additional fees may apply if the student's voucher does not fully cover the total tuition and miscellaneous fees.

### Step 3: Registrar's Office

1. After the requirements have been verified and the necessary payments have been made, proceed to the *Registrar's Office*.
2. The Registrar will encode the student's information into the school system.
3. The student will be assigned to a section based on the school's sectioning guidelines.

### Step 4: Confirmation of Enrollment and ID Picture

1. Once all enrollment requirements have been completed, the student's enrollment form will be stamped *"Officially Enrolled."*
2. The student will then proceed to the designated area for *ID picture-taking*.

# Senior High School Voucher Program

The Senior High School Voucher Program provides financial assistance to qualified students enrolled in private Senior High Schools.

### Voucher Amount

* *Voucher value:* ₱17,500.00

---

# Eligibility for Automatic Voucher Coverage

A student is automatically eligible for the voucher if:

* The student graduated from *Grade 10 in a public school*.
* The student immediately enrolls in *Grade 11 at a private school*.
* There is *no gap year or interruption* between Grade 10 completion and Grade 11 enrollment.

Students who meet these requirements do not need to apply separately for the voucher.

---

# Students Who Need to Apply Through PEAC

A student must personally apply through the *PEAC Online Voucher Application Portal (OVAP)* if:

* The student graduated from a public school but *stopped studying for one year or more* before enrolling in Grade 11 at a private school.

In this case, voucher approval is subject to PEAC's evaluation and approval process.

---

# Important Transfer Guidelines

### Transferring from a Public School to a Private School in Grade 12

* Students who completed or started *Grade 11 in a public school* and transfer to a private school for *Grade 12* are *not eligible* for the voucher program.

### Transferring from One Private School to Another

* Students who started *Grade 11 in a private school* and already have an approved voucher may transfer to another private school.
* The voucher may be transferred, subject to existing guidelines and requirements.

# Enrollment Incentives and Promotions

### Grade 11 Early Enrollment Promo

Students who enroll within the enrollment period specified by Northlink are eligible for the following incentive:

* *Early Enrollment Promo + Voucher = Zero Tuition Balance*

This means qualified students who meet the promo requirements may have no remaining tuition balance after the voucher has been applied.

### Grade 12 Referral Incentive

Grade 12 students who successfully refer at least one new student to enroll at Northlink are eligible for the following incentive:

* *Referral Incentive + Voucher = Zero Tuition Balance*

Terms and conditions for the referral program apply.
